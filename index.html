Creating Space for People of Color in Technology.

About Us

ROOTs Technology is a community curated by people of color, for people of color in the Austin area who want to learn the basics of web development.

 

ROOTs Technology provides space for people of color to learn the basics of web development through free monthly workshops. In addition to gaining hands-on experience and practice, students will learn more about the technology industry from an array of mentors, speakers, and partnerships with various technology companies in the Austin area.
