About Us


ROOTs Technology is a tutoring and mentoring service that helps beginners learn web development basics.

Being self-taught engineers ourselves, we've curated a curriculum of online interactive resources to help beginners start learning to code at their own pace. We help facilitate their learning through mentoring, tutoring sessions, video tutorials, and interview prep.

Our curriculum and tutoring services are a cheaper alternative to expensive coding bootcamps, providing an affordable option for more students to have access.
